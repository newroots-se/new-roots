Hello! Welcome to newroots, a human-centred, inclusive platform that brings together step-by-step guidance and essential information needed for settling into a new life in Sweden, in one simple, intuitive experience.

Whether you are planning on moving to Sweden, have just arrived, or if you have already settled, you will find all the essential information you need - plus useful related information, to help you on your journey.

We value your opinion and have built in a simple way for relaying feedback. Feel free to use it, if you think it might help others navigating similar situations.

Beyond pure guidance, we are developing New Roots to be a personalised experience, and a destination where internationals can connect, share and thrive in Sweden within a safe digital community. We are also working to bring other culturally deep, rich experiences to the platform.
